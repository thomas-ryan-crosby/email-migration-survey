⭐ Our recommendation

No strong preference? Use Gmail.

If you don't have a favorite email app, we recommend Gmail at gmail.com on your computer and the Gmail app on your phone. Since your email now lives on Google Workspace, the Google apps are the most direct, reliable fit — same company on both ends, nothing extra to configure.

If you'd rather stick with what you already use (Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.), keep scrolling — those guides are below.
